About MCMF : The Assessment Test

In India students prepare through day and night to get into premier institutes whether they are inclined towards it or not. In India, the choice of a course or a career path of a student/individual is significantly influenced by family and friends. In most cases it is observed that the students end up taking courses on the suggestion/advice from others which they are not interested in and find it difficult to cope with it. Choosing a course/career path by understanding his/her personality, interest and passion will be more meaningful and enriching. MCMF aims at helping these young people to understand their strengths and choose a career path that matches their personality.

Instructions for the Assessment Test

  • Approach the test with an open mind

  • Be truthfull while answering the questions

  • Do not try to validate an already choosen or persuing career path

  • Read the questions properly before answering

  • The time limit for the assessment test is 40 minutes
