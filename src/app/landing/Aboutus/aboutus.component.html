What Miligen Technologies stands for?

  • Optimization of Supply Chain of the Education sector

  • Proving Mentoring is as important as Tutoring in the information age

  • Breaking Cognitive Biases of the society about career choices

  • Creating an environment where individuals can take control of thier own career

  • Providing opportunity to students to make informed career decisions

What RavSpace is as a product?

  • Not just counselling but End-to-End Career Assistance

  • Make an Informed Career Choice with the help of assessment test and career guides

  • Validate your Career with industry experts before you start putting efforts

  • Take help from a Mentor from the Field of choice in your path to Success
